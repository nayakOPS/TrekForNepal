Trek & Expedition's We Offer

Embark on thrilling treks, conquer majestic peaks, explore rich cultural heritage, encounter wildlife on jungle safaris, indulge in adventure sports, and customize your trip for a truly unforgettable experience. Join us for the adventure of a lifetime.

Trekking

Embark on a journey of a lifetime as you traverse ancient trails, lush valleys, and majestic peaks. Choose from our diverse range of trekking packages, carefully crafted to suit different skill levels and durations. Our knowledgeable guides will lead you through iconic routes like Everest Base Camp, Annapurna Circuit, and Langtang Valley, ensuring your safety and comfort every step of the way.

Adventure Sports

Unleash your adventurous spirit with our adrenaline-pumping adventure sports activities. Plunge into the rapids of white-water rafting, soar through the skies with paragliding, or challenge your limits with bungee jumping and canyoning. Explore the rugged terrain of Nepal on thrilling mountain biking trails. Prepare for an adrenaline-fueled experience like no other.

Peak Climbing

For the thrill-seekers and mountaineering enthusiasts, we offer exhilarating peak climbing expeditions. Conquer the breathtaking summits of peaks such as Island Peak, Mera Peak, and Lobuche Peak, accompanied by our experienced climbing guides. We provide top-notch equipment, permits, and logistical support to make your ascent a memorable achievement.
